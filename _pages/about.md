About me
======
I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the 
[Department of Computer & Information Sciences at Towson University](https://www.towson.edu/fcsm/departments/computerinfosci/), 
supervised by 
[Prof. Xin Huang](https://www.towson.edu/fcsm/departments/computerinfosci/facultystaff/xin-huang.html). 
I previously had research internships at 
[Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health, Department of Biostatistics](https://publichealth.jhu.edu/departments/biostatistics), 
under the supervision of 
[Prof. Ni Zhao](https://publichealth.jhu.edu/faculty/3331/ni-zhao), 
and at the 
[Institute of Advanced Computing and Digital Engineering, Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences](https://szs.siat.ac.cn/), 
supervised by 
[Prof. Ruxin Wang](https://people.ucas.ac.cn/~rxwang). 
I received my B.Eng. degree in 
[Artificial Intelligence, School of Intelligent Systems Science and Engineering, Jinan University](https://sisse.jnu.edu.cn/).
